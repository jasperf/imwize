Types of WordPress Hosting

Shared Hosting

You are just getting started. You need hosting, are on a tight budget and or have little or no knowledge of hosting. You get what you pay for is the saying.

VPS, Cloud, DVPS, Dedicated

You want more freedom, more power and with more power comes more responsability you realize. But that is your thing as you crave more control.

Managed Hosting

You need reliable, fast, managed hosting, don't want to worry about your site too much and budget is not a major issue.

What to Look For

Ram, Storage, Bandwidth, Staging, Backups

You need to have enough RAM, storage and OK bandwidth to run your site. Backups and staging area built in? This besides the general requirements to run WordPress When running caching plugins RAM is often vital and many hosters charge little for storage and focus on RAM instead. Dreamhost is an example

Customer Care and or Management

Customer Care is really important and often hosters that offer cheap packages hardly have any. Customer care to me means a good knowledge base, quick and thorough answers to tickets opened and knowledge of WordPress. Live chat is an added advantage.

Location | CDN

The physical location of your server is really important. When your client is in the Middle East a West Coast US Server might not be a good idea. With the help of CDNs you can mitigate this issue and serve a more global crowd.

The Options out there

Dreamhost, Bluehost, Siteground, ..

Great WordPress Specialized Hosters. Bluehost is still no 1. NB Some of these offer VPS, Dedicated and other too.

Digital Ocean, Amazon, Heroku, Siteground..

Hosters with cloud packages Packages that are easily scaleable and with full access. Digital Ocean is my and many developers favorite cloud hoster

WP Engine, Pagely, Synthesis, ..

Well known managed hosting options where the hoster takes care of the management of your site, helps with updates and malware scans and mitigation and so on.

Don't go for cheap and easy Go for great hosters that know WordPress like their inside pocket.
